Deliciously Rich Chocolate Cake

A Very Merry [un]Birthday Recipe!

I'm just gonna say it: I will never tire of this cake. It's rich, perfectly moist and beautifully
simple in a way that all the best things are.

Not only is this cake a delicious staple to bust out for birthdays and other celebrations,
but it also makes for a pretty incredible morning-after snack (best served with humility and
a steaming mug of coffee).

Ingredients

  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder (sifted)
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. Stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt in large bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  6. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
  7. Wait 10 minutes, remove cakes from pans to wire racks and allow to cool completely.
  8. Frost with our favorite chocolate frosting, and enjoy!
